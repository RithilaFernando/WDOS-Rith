SERVICES

Our hospital offers a comprehensive range of specialized healthcare services, ensuring expert care and advanced treatment. From emergency care and cardiology to pediatrics and maternity services, we cater to patients at every stage of life. We also provide specialized support in oncology, orthopedics, radiology, physical therapy, neurology, dermatology, and mental health, all delivered with a focus on compassion and excellence.


Emergency Care

Immediate treatment for life-threatening conditions.

Cardiology

Comprehensive heart care including diagnostics and treatments.

Pediatrics

Specialized medical care for infants, children, and adolescents.

Maternity Services

Complete care for expecting mothers and newborns.

Surgical Services

Advanced surgical procedures with expert care.

Oncology

Specialized treatment and support for cancer patients.

Orthopedics

Care for bone, joint, and muscle conditions.

Radiology

State-of-the-art imaging services for accurate diagnoses.

Physical Therapy

Rehabilitation services to restore mobility and function.

Neurology

Expert care for brain, spinal cord, and nerve disorders.

Dermatology

Treatment for skin conditions and diseases.

Mental Health Services

Comprehensive support for mental and emotional well-being.
